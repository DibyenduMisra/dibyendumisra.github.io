About Me

I am an aspiring Planetary Scientist focused on understanding the thermal evolution of the Moon through remote sensing-based global characterization of lunar pyroclastic deposits.

My research integrates VIS–NIR reflectance spectroscopy, radiative transfer modelling, Monte Carlo simulations, and statistical inference–based spectral unmixing, along with telescope-based multi-band imaging polarimetry, to investigate lunar volcanic deposits.
